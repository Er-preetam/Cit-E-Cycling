Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cit-E Cycling?

Cit-E Cycling is a premier national cycling competition spanning ten mini pop-up tournaments across major UK cities. Participants ride on high-tech smart exercise bikes equipped with precision sensors that track distance and average power output in real-time.

How do I participate?

Simply navigate to our Registration Page, select your local city tournament, fill in your details, and book your preferred 1-hour time slot. Booking in advance is highly recommended to secure your place.

Is there an entry fee?

No, participation in the Cit-E Cycling challenge is completely free! However, bike availability is limited, so pre-booking a specific time slot is mandatory for all competitors.

What equipment do I need to bring?

No special equipment is required! We provide the pro-grade smart exercise bikes and all necessary tracking software. We simply recommend wearing comfortable sports clothing and athletic footwear for your ride.

How are the winners decided?

A live leaderboard tracks average power output and total distance covered by every participant. Riders are ranked by city, age category, and best performance. The highest scorers at the end of the city event receive tournament trophies and city glory!
